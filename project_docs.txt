## üïπÔ∏è Game Logic Breakdown

### 1. Ludo King (Royal Edition)
- **Logic**: Implements full Ludo board rules (15x15 grid).
- **AI System**: A priority-based scoring algorithm (`calculateBestMove`):
  - **Tier 1 (1,000,000 pts)**: Move that reaches HOME.
  - **Tier 2 (500,000 pts)**: Move that hits/captures an opponent's piece.
  - **Tier 3 (300,000 pts)**: Deploying a new piece from the base.
  - **Tier 4 (100,000 pts)**: Landing on a "Safe Spot" (Star).
- **Mechanics**: Space/Enter to roll, 1-4 keys to select pieces.

### 2. Ball Maze (Precision Movement)
- **Logic**: Canvas-based rendering with grid collision.
- **Features**: 
  - **Fog of War**: A radial gradient hides parts of the maze.
  - **Magnet Power-up**: Uses distance-based attraction to pull coins toward the player.
  - **Moving Walls**: Level 5+ introduces dynamic obstacles that bounce between walls.
- **Infinite Play**: Levels advance indefinitely; time is unlimited.

### 3. Bird Fly (Physics-based)
- **Logic**: Simple gravity and velocity simulation (`requestAnimationFrame`).
- **Mechanics**: Tap/Space to apply upward force. Score increases as you pass through golden pillars.

### 4. Ghost Kill (Reflex)
- **Logic**: Time-based spawning. Ghosts appear at random coordinates; users must click/hover to "slay" them before they vanish.
